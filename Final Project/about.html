The goth Hippie Story

The Goth Hippie began as a passion project for our foundress, Laura Ayala, as she endeavored to live a cleaner, greener life, without sacrificing her sense of style. The term emerged when she was asked by a friend to describe her personal style; think Morticia Addams with equal parts Frida Kahlo and Janis Joplin.

Blend that style with a fascination of natural beauty products, and thus The Goth Hippie was born. Through close collaborations with the top beauty brands and bloggers, we have curated the best beauty products to create our ESSENTIALS Collection, a painless gateway into natural beauty products for our strange women.



The Foundress

Laura Ayala is a proud Latina born and raised in New York City. Her desire to launch a beauty+wellness brand began several years after her PCOS diagnosis at the age of sixteen. An increasingly common hormonal disorder, the syndrome is aggravated by poor diet and, little to her knowledge, topical products loaded with endocrine-disrupting toxins.

Seven years later, Laura maintains a diet rich in whole foods and indulges in natural beauty rituals. And she is sharing those years of trial and error in the form of The Goth Hippie Essentials Collection. Skin, hair, lips, and brows - the essentials to put your best face forward.

"For all my spooky sisters, you don't have to sacrifice your #BrujaGlam style to live clean. Eat clean, drink tons of water, and know what you're putting in and on your body. Es así de fácil.
-Besos, Laura "
